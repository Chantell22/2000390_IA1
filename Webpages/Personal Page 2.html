What's My Personality

In my opinion personality test are insightful and interesting way to categorise and identify aspects about your behaviour. However, I do not think personality test are completely accurate. Personality tests are mostly dependent on a series of question (usually 60) ranging in degree of certainty which are used to identify the intricates of a personâ€™s personality. But these questions are a self-evaluation. That means that the test subject is directly responsible for the outcome. People can perhaps lie to themselves or repress an idea or emotion. Furthermore, mental trauma and mental illness can cause individuals to become unbalanced in their emotions and perceptions. Thus, whereas personality test is important groundwork their validity is one hundred percent unbased.
